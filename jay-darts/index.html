JAY DARTS

Pull back the dart like a slingshot, aim the laser carefully, and smash the bullseye! Reach 200 points to win.

HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Touch the dart and pull it BACKWARD like a slingshot.
🎯 The green laser will point FORWARD to show your target.
🎯 Release your finger to throw.
🎯 Yellow Center (Bullseye) = 20 Points.
🎯 Outer Rings = 15, 10, 5 Points.
🎯 Score 200 Points to WIN!
