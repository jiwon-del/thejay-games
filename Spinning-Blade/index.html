[ HOW TO PLAY MANUAL ]

• PC Controls: Move your Mouse Cursor across the screen. The center core of the spinning blade is fully pinned and will directly follow your cursor position.

• Mobile Controls: Touch and Drag anywhere inside the canvas area to map the blade directly under your tracking finger.

• Objective: Use the revolving outer blades to crush incoming enemies. Protect the blue center body from getting hit to stay alive!
