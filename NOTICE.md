# NOTICE

## Games Submission Reward Distribution

To ensure successful reward distribution for the Games submission campaign, all participants are required to submit their reward wallet address in the **#jay-rewards** channel on our Discord server.

### Required Action

* Post the wallet address where you wish to receive your Games submission rewards.
* Make sure the address is correct and accessible.
* Only addresses submitted through the designated **#jay-rewards** channel will be considered for reward distribution.

### Important

Failure to submit your reward address may result in delays or inability to receive rewards. Please complete this step as soon as possible.

Thank you for your participation and support of The Jay Network.
