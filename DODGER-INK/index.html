DODGER INK

Neon Arcade Edition
🎮 HOW TO PLAY MANUAL
1
Draw Lines: Click & drag your mouse (on PC) or swipe across the screen (on Mobile) to ink bright purple neon lines.
2
Bounce the Ball: Use your drawn lines as trampolines to deflect and keep the glowing ball airborne.
3
Avoid Spikes: Do not let the ball drop into the sharp grey spikes at the bottom of the screen, or it's Game Over!
4
Score Points: Collect the rotating ★ Glowing Stars to earn +20 points for each catch. Lines will fade shortly after a bounce!
